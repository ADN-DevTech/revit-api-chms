Remarks
The position of the point is given by CoordinateSystem.Origin, and the orientation is specified by the three unit vectors CoordinateSystem.BasisX, BasisY, BasisZ. The basis vectors must be unit length and mutually perpendicular. Whenever the Reference property is not , changing the CoordinateSystem property has a compound effect. First the point is moved to the specified location. Then the point is moved to conform to its Reference, by the shortest possible distance.
