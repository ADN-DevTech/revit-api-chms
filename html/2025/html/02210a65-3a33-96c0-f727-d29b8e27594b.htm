Exceptions
ExceptionCondition
ArgumentException For rectangular and oval parts the axis rotation must be a multiple of PI/2. -or- The connector does not belong to a fabrication part. -or- Connector is not connected. -or- Connector belongs to a fabrication part tap. -or- Fabrication part cannot be rotated because it is either unable to be re-sized or it is too constrained.
ArgumentNullException A non-optional argument was null
InvalidOperationException rotate failed because the fabrication part geometry could not be modified accordingly.
Remarks
Attempts to resize rectangular and oval ends if the angle is a multiple of PI/2. Taps cannot be rotated. For rectangular and oval profiles only 90, 180 and 270 degree rotations are valid.
